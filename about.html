About

I am Nwoye Ezekiel, an undergraduate student in the department of Computer Engineering at the University of Lagos(UNILAG), Nigeria.

I started as a Graphic Designer and due to the great passion and deep interest i had in designing, I developed the urge to explore more into the design field which led me to study more and undergo self training to become a UI/UX designer and a Frontend web developer.

I love programming and the fundamental cause of which i enjoy being a Frontend web developer is the fact that i have the ability to bring my ideas and imaginations into reality through an app/website, providing solutions to real life problems.
